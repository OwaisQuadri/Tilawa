## Navigation & Jump Sheet UX

### B1: Accurate Page Jump
- Open the Jump Sheet (tap page number)
- Navigate to Position tab
- Select a surah like Al-Kahf (18) and ayah 1
- Tap "Go"
- Verify the app lands on the exact page where Surah 18:1 appears (should be precise, not ±1-2 pages off)
- Test with multiple surahs (especially those with variable page boundaries)

### B2: Bookmarks & Recents Tabs
- **Bookmarks**: Long-press any ayah, tap "Bookmark Ayah"
  - Open Jump Sheet → Bookmarks tab
  - Verify the bookmarked ayah appears with its label and surah/ayah reference
  - Try the three sort options: Newest, Oldest, Position
  - Swipe left on a bookmark to delete it
  - Tap a bookmark to jump to that ayah
- **Recents**: Open Jump Sheet → Recents tab
  - Verify it shows a combined list of recent jumps (from using the jump sheet) and listening sessions
  - Each entry shows a jump icon or headphones icon to distinguish the type
  - Tap any entry to jump to that ayah
- **Bookmark Toggle**: Long-press an already-bookmarked ayah
  - Verify the alert shows "Remove Bookmark" (destructive role) instead of "Bookmark Ayah"
  - Tap to remove the bookmark, then re-bookmark to verify toggle works

### B3: Fuzzy Search in Jump Sheet
- Open Jump Sheet and tap the search bar
- Test patterns:
  - "10:10" or "10 10" → should show Surah 10:10
  - "p 300" or "page 300" → should show Page 300
  - "j 5" or "juz 5" → should show Juz 5
  - "100" (bare number) → should show Page 100, Surah 100:1, and all surahs with ayah 100
  - "yunus" or "kahf" → fuzzy match on surah names, tap to jump
- Verify search results appear as a list
- Tap any result to jump to that location
- Clear the search box to return to normal tab view
