## Recording Auto-Naming by Date & Time

- Open Library tab and start a new microphone recording
- Record some audio, then stop
- Verify the recording title defaults to the current date and time (e.g. "Mar 8, 2026, 3:42 PM") instead of "New Recording"
- Tap the recording to open its detail view and verify the auto-generated title is displayed
- Tap edit (pencil icon) and change the title to a custom name, save
- Create another recording and verify it also gets an auto-generated date/time title
- Verify that each new recording has a unique date/time title matching when it was created
