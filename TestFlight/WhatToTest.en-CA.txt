## Jump-to Recents Persistence

- Open the Mushaf
- Tap the Jump-to button at the top
- Click on a Recent entry (or navigate to any ayah to create a new one)
- Force-quit the app (swipe up in app switcher)
- Relaunch the app
- Tap Jump-to again and check the Recents tab
- Verify your recent jump appears in the list (not lost after force-quit)
