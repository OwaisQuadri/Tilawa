## CDN Download & Cache Management

- Navigate to a reciter with CDN sources → tap Manage CDN (renamed from "Download Surahs")
- Verify surahs show per-surah progress bars while downloading, green checkmarks when done
- Tap menu (ellipsis) → "Download All" downloads all uncached surahs in background
- Navigate away and back to Manage CDN — download progress persists and updates
- Tap menu → "Share CDN" and verify it opens share sheet with CDN URL
- Download some surahs, then tap menu → "Clear Cache" → confirm deletion removes only that CDN's cached files
- Verify other reciters' cached CDN files are unaffected by the cache clear
- Tap "Check for Updates" — availability check runs with progress indicator
- If uploading a CDN: complete upload, verify new version number increments
- Disable Wi-Fi, verify "Check for Updates" gracefully handles network errors (no crash)
