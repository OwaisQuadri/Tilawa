- Browse and play Quran recitations from a selection of reciters.
- Download reciter audio for a surah to listen offline.
- Import personal recordings from audio or video files via the Library tab.
- Import audio from YouTube by pasting a video link — download progress is shown inline with a stop button to cancel.
- Annotate a recording with ayah markers to navigate by verse during playback.
- Manage which reciters appear in playback using the priority list in the Library tab.
- Switch reciters and surahs from the playback screen without leaving the player.
