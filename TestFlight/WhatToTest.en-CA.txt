## Annotation Editor Redesign

- Open a recording and tap Edit — the editor should fill the full screen with a waveform at the top
- The bottom toolbar has 6 buttons: Auto (sparkles), Undo, Redo, Play/Pause, Cut (scissors), and Add Marker (plus.circle)
- Add a marker, tap the pencil icon at the top — a sheet opens to assign ayah or marker type (Ayah/Crop After/Crop Before)
- Enable "Auto" toggle and add markers — they should auto-assign types: after CropAfter comes CropBefore, after CropBefore comes Ayah
- Place CropAfter and CropBefore markers — purple shading appears between them on the waveform indicating the crop region
- Tap the Cut button with a crop region active — the audio is immediately cut, a "Cropping selections…" spinner appears, the Cut operation can be undone
- Tap Undo/Redo — the cut is reversed or reapplied; waveform and markers update
- Assign two markers with only start ayahs and a gap between them, then tap Finalize — an alert warns that this is irreversible and will remove the gap
- Tap Finalize — segments are kept, gaps (like rakaah breaks) are removed, file size is reduced, markers are adjusted to new positions
- Pinch horizontally on the waveform — zoom in/out; drag the waveform to scrub; playback follows the cursor or resumes from scrub position
