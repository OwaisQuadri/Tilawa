## Sliding Window & Mini Player Indicators

### Sliding Window Mode
- Open Play sheet and select a range (e.g., Fatiha 1:1-1:7)
- Switch to "Sliding Window" mode and tap Play
- Mini player should show: phase icon (□ for solo/connection, ■ for full range) + position (e.g. "1/7") + ayah repeat counter (e.g. "2/5") + range repeat counter (e.g. "1/3")
- After repeating completes, verify the arrow icon shows (e.g. "3a" for continue 3 ayahs)
- Press skip forward/back buttons - verify counters reset correctly to 1 when moving between phases
- Press pause/play and verify the mini player responds correctly

### Standard Repeat Mode
- Open Play sheet, set "Each verse" to 5× and "Range" to 3×
- Tap Play on a 3-ayah range
- Mini player should show: phase icon (□ for solo when repeating ayahs, ■ otherwise) + position in queue (e.g. "2/3" when 3 ayahs)
- As you play through, verify phase icon stays □ while ayahs are repeating, then switches to ■ for range repeats
- Press skip forward/back - verify position counter updates and resets correctly
- Verify after-repeat indicator shows when configured (e.g. "5a" for continue 5 ayahs)

### After-Repeat Continuation (Both Modes)
- Set "After repeating" to "5 ayahs" in either mode
- Play a range and complete all repeats
- Verify mini player shows the continuation indicator (arrow icon) with short label (e.g. "5a")
- Play to completion and verify it continues to the next 5 ayahs as expected

### Skip & Stop Button Fixes
- During any playback, tap skip forward/back buttons
- Verify the mini player mini player controls respond immediately (buttons should no longer be unresponsive)
- Tap stop (X button) at any time - playback should stop and mini player should disappear
- Start a new session immediately after stopping - verify you can override and start a different playback mode
